How to Generate an Invoice
1
Open the invoice form
Click the "Generate Invoice" button below to open the order entry form. You can configure the company logo, Facebook page link, and thank-you message from the form as well.
2
Select product SKU
Choose a SKU from the dropdown. The product name will auto-fill immediately. You can add multiple product lines to a single invoice.
3
Enter quantity and price
Set the quantity and unit price (BDT). The subtotal and grand total update automatically in real time.
4
Fill in customer details
Enter the customer name, contact number, and delivery address. These are required to generate the invoice.
5
Preview and edit inline
A live preview of the invoice appears. Click on any bold value inside the invoice to make quick inline edits before downloading.
6
Download as A4 PDF
Click "Download PDF" to save a print-ready black and white A4 invoice. The invoice number increments automatically with each download.
